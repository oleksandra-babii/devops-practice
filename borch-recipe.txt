1. Prepare ingredients: beets, cabbage, potatoes, carrots, onions, garlic, dill, soul cream, vinegar, tomato paste, and spices.
2. Boil beef with salt for 60–90 minutes.
3. Grate beets, mix with vinegar and tomato paste, cook in pan until soft.
4. Sauté chopped onion and grated carrot in oil until soft.
5. Add cubed potatoes to broth, cook 5–7 minutes.
